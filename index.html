I'm an enthusiastic full-stack web developer with a goal to create meaningful web applications that are fun to use by focusing on user-centric design. Let’s connect so we can talk about how I can bring my passion my to your project!

Since graduating from The State University of New York’s Polytechnic Institute in 2015, I worked as an application engineer for two years focusing on SQL and Magic/Unipaas. Although the back-end was facinating, I wanted to find a position that required more creativity. I moved from Connecticut to California to take General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive course where and love learning about web development because my interest in coding and design converge.

When not immersed in web development, I can be found looking for new ways to stay active in my new city of San Francisco. I’m happiest when I’m around family, friends, or someone I can learn from. I used to dress my cat up every Halloween, until the cat decided to live with my father in New York because he feeds her hot dogs and ice cream. Now, I stay happy dressing up webpages.
